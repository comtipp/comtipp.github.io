Understanding Social Tipping Points Through Computational Modelling

COMTIP is a collaborative research initiative between the University of Amsterdam's Computational Science Lab, POLDER Simulation Center, and the Dutch National Institute for Public Health and the Environment (RIVM). Our mission is to develop advanced computational models to understand and predict social tipping points in complex societal systems.
